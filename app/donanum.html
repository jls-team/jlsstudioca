First step

Observation and idea

There are many skill-share like sites, but there is no site to find tutors or mentors who teach in person. Especially for beginners, it's hard to describe problems they are facing. I remember when I was in University there was post boards in a school to find tutors. The main goal of the site is help people to find tutors or mentors via online post-board site.
