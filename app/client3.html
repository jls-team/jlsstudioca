PayShepherd

Project

Design consulting for web platform

Summary

PayShepherd is an advanced contractor billing platform that effectively eliminates the occurrence of numerous billing discrepancies each year. By automating the traditional "stare and compare" approach, PayShepherd compares billing data against the original contracts, swiftly detecting any inconsistencies and promptly alerting contractors. This enables seamless communication to clarify the reasoning behind the discrepancies or rectify any oversights. As the company continues to experience rapid growth, there is a desire to revamp the platform's design and enhance user-friendliness to better serve its expanding user base.
